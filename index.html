About Prabhleen Arora

Prabhleen Arora has a Master in Clinical Psychology and over 5 years of experience supporting personal transformation.
Her practice serves individuals, couples, and community members across healthcare, education, and corporate life. She specializes in stress, anxiety, trauma, relationship, and sexuality issues, and LGBTQIA+ affirmative therapy.
She is deeply committed to holistic well-being, blending evidence-based therapeutic methods with genuine empathy. She offers a non-judgmental environment for healing, helping clients explore their authentic selves with resilience and courage. Her sessions build insight, emotional strength, and lasting change tailored to each journey.
