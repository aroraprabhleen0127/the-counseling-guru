About Prabhleen Arora

Prabhleen Arora has a Master in Clinical Psychology and over 5 years of experience supporting personal transformation.
Her practice serves a wide variety of clients—individuals, couples, and community members—across healthcare, education, and corporate life. She specializes in stress, anxiety, trauma, relationship and sexuality issues, and LGBTQIA+ affirmative therapy.
Prabhleen is deeply committed to holistic well-being, blending evidence-based therapeutic methods with genuine empathy. She offers a non-judgmental environment for healing, helping every client explore their authentic self with resilience and courage. Her sessions build insight, emotional strength, and lasting change tailored to each journey.

Our Counselling Services

  • Individual Counselling: Personalized support to overcome anxiety, depression, trauma, self-esteem challenges, and tough life transitions—empowering healthy growth and emotional stability.
  • Couples Therapy: Relationship-focused sessions that help partners rebuild trust, resolve conflicts, enhance communication, and nurture intimacy—for all couples, from newly together to long-term marriages.
  • LGBTQIA+ Affirmative Therapy: Safe and supportive counselling for LGBTQIA+ individuals and relationships, addressing identity, coming out, social stigma, mental health, and love with deep understanding and care.
